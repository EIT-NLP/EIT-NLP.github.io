Join Us

The group focuses on cultivating innovative and entrepreneurial top talents with the spirit of science, providing superior research conditions, generous salary, broad development space, diversified international academic exchanges as well as opportunities for exchanges and cooperation with well-known enterprises. We are now inviting young talents, outstanding scholars or exchange students with relevant research backgrounds from home and abroad to join us. Job Responsibilities

  1. Our team is committed to developing cutting-edge machine learning and speech processing technologies to optimize customer problem solutions and enhance customer service.
  2. We advocate fast action and experimentation, focusing on iteration and scaling, while making a thoughtful balance between speed and quality.
  3. Research is one of the core pursuits of our team. We maintain close cooperation with many universities at home and abroad, and have a top team to provide research support and guidance. We encourage research to improve the performance of our products, and present our research results in top conferences as one of our works.

Job Description

  1. Apply state-of-the-art neural network technology to design solutions for specific business problems.
  2. Identify and overcome the limitations of existing models, and develop and promote industry-leading product models.
  3. Track industry technology trends and work with engineering teams to provide technical solutions based on product planning.
  4. Independently apply for research project funds, assist the subject group in writing and declaring funds, and can independently undertake to carry out subjects, design experiments and reporting work.

Recruitment Positions

Postdoctoral Fellow, Assistant Researcher, Associate Researcher

With or about to obtain a doctoral degree in science and technology, aged 35 and below.

  • Comprehensive annual salary of 400,000-600,000 RMB before tax (including postdoctoral living allowance from local government, and one-off negotiation for those who are especially outstanding);
  • Enjoy welfare benefits including five insurance, one gold, paid annual leave, holiday benefits, and priority support to apply for government talent apartments (turnkey);
  • During the period of the station, you can apply to the local government for a maximum of 200,000 yuan of research funding, and can apply for various types of talent projects and the National Natural Science Foundation of China as the person in charge; for those who have received funding from the China Postdoctoral Science Fund and provincial postdoctoral research projects, Ningbo will provide a 1:1 matching funding support.

Algorithm Engineer

Master's degree or above, under 35 years old, experience in large factories is preferred.

Research Intern

Undergraduate or master's degree student, able to intern for 6 months and above is preferred.
