Join Us

Our laboratory focuses on cultivating excellent scientific research talents. In addition to providing excellent research conditions and generous salary, we also provide opportunities for international academic exchanges and cooperation with enterprises. We sincerely invite domestic and foreign scholars with relevant computer research background to join us. See below for the specific positions and benefits.

Laboratory Vision

  1. Our team is committed to developing cutting-edge machine learning, natural language processing, and multimodal big model technologies to optimize real-world problem solutions.
  2. We advocate fast action and experimentation, with a focus on iteration and scaling, while striking a thoughtful balance between speed and quality.
  3. Research is one of the core pursuits of our team. We maintain close cooperation with many universities at home and abroad, and have a top team to provide research support and guidance. We encourage research to improve the performance of our products, and present our research results in top conferences as one of our works.

Work Content

  1. Apply state-of-the-art neural network technology to design solutions for specific business problems.
  2. Identify and overcome the limitations of existing models, and develop and promote industry-leading product models.
  3. Track industry technology trends, and collaborate with engineering teams to create impactful and practical solutions.
  4. Independently apply for research project funds, assist the project team to write and declare funds, and can independently undertake to carry out the project, design experiments and reporting work.

Recruitment Positions

Postdoctoral Fellow, Assistant Researcher, Associate Researcher

Requirements

  1. Have or will soon obtain a doctoral degree in computer science, artificial intelligence and other related fields;
  2. Have good reading, communication and writing skills in English;
  3. Passionate about research, responsible and teamwork spirit, and strong research ability;
  4. Have published high-level academic papers or representative results in the field of artificial intelligence, NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CPVR, ACL and other top computer-related conference papers are preferred;
  5. Priority will be given to those who have presided over national, provincial and ministerial level research projects;
  6. Be proficient in TensorFlow, PyTorch, Git and other related frameworks and tools, and have the ability to independently carry out algorithm design and model optimization;

Salary

  1. Comprehensive annual salary of 400,000-600,000 RMB before tax (including local government postdoctoral living subsidy, and one-off negotiation for those who are particularly outstanding);
  2. Enjoy welfare benefits, including five insurance, one gold, paid annual leave, holiday benefits, and priority support for the application of government talent apartment (turnkey);
  3. During the period of the station can apply to the local government for a maximum of 200,000 yuan of scientific research funding, can be the person in charge of applying for various types of talent projects and the National Natural Science Foundation of China; for those who have received funding from the China Postdoctoral Science Fund and provincial postdoctoral research projects, Ningbo City will give 1:1 matching funding support.

Internship

Requirements

  1. Able to participate in the scientific research work in the laboratory for a long time (more than 6 months);
  2. Active thinking, innovative, strong enthusiasm for technological innovation and research and development, strong sense of responsibility, hardworking;
  3. Strong programming skills are preferred, and experience in deep learning or natural language processing and other related research is preferred.

Algorithm Engineer

Requirements

  1. Master's degree or above, background in computer science, artificial intelligence or related fields is preferred.
  2. Master the basics of deep learning, familiar with at least one deep learning programming framework, such as PyTorch, etc., with experience in model development, deployment, and fine-tuning;
  3. Good programming style and document writing ability, have multi-person collaborative development experience, have strict quality requirements for the code; familiar with common development specifications, familiar with Git and other development tools, familiar with the creation and maintenance of code libraries.
  4. Have a good grasp of the basics of natural language processing, with relevant practical or research experience; have an in-depth understanding of multimodal large models, information retrieval, model compression or reasoning and practical experience is preferred;
  5. Timely follow-up of cutting-edge technology progress, excellent problem analysis and solving ability and teamwork ability.

How to Apply

Application materials should include: CV (named as "Job Title - Name - Major - School"), with a description of the research direction of interest, relevant work or project experience, and relevant information proving my ability and level; meanwhile, send the above application materials to e-mail: xyshen@eitech.edu.cn.
