Our Team

We are a vibrant team led by Xiaoyu Shen—including PhDs, research engineers, and interns—comes together with a shared passion for advancing natural language processing (NLP). We're dedicated to developing innovative algorithms that enable machines to truly understand, generate, and reason with human language.

PI Introduction

Xiaoyu Shen

Xiaoyu Shen graduated from Nanjing University School of Software in 2015 as an undergraduate, and then received his PhD degree from Max Planck Institute for Information Research and Saarland University Graduate School in Germany, under the supervision of Gerhard Weikum and Dietrich Klakow. His research interests mainly include hidden variable Bayesian modeling, dialog generation, Q&A systems, etc.

In September 2020, he joined Amazon Alexa AI Berlin R&D Center as a Machine Learning Scientist position, leading the Alexa Intelligent Customer Service Merchandise Q&A project. He joined Oriental Institute of Technology Advanced Research Institute in November 2023 as an Associate Researcher and Ph.D. Supervisor, jointly cultivating Ph.D. students with Shanghai Jiaotong University, China University of Science and Technology and Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

He has published several papers in top conferences on artificial intelligence and natural language processing, such as ACL, ICML, EMNLP, ICSE. He has won the National Scholarship, Outstanding Graduate of Nanjing University, Outstanding Graduation Design, National Outstanding Overseas Privately Financed Students, COLING Best Demo Paper Award, ACL Best Topic Paper Award, and CIS-RAM Best Paper Award Nomination.

He has given many invited presentations at the University of Tokyo, Cambridge University, Google Brain, etc. He is a committee member of several top conferences and journals including ACL, EMNLP, AISTAS, NeurIPS, TOIS, a domain chair of ACL Q&A Direction, and a theme editor of High-Performance Computing for AI in Big Model Era.
