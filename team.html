Our Team

We are a vibrant team led by Xiaoyu Shen—including PhDs, research engineers, and interns—comes together with a shared passion for advancing natural language processing (NLP). We're dedicated to developing innovative algorithms that enable machines to truly understand, generate, and reason with human language.

PI Introduction

Xiaoyu Shen

Xiaoyu Shen, Assistant Professor and Ph.D. Supervisor in the Department of Informatics, received his B.S. degree from Nanjing University (2015) and Ph.D. degree from Max Planck Institute for Computer Science, Germany (2020). He studied under ACM fellow Gerhard Weikum and Dietrich Klakow. after his PhD graduation, he worked as a machine learning scientist at Amazon Alexa AI (2020-2023), and led the development of Amazon's intelligent customer service Q&A system. His main research interests include hidden variable Bayesian modeling, text generation, Q&A, fine-tuning and multimodal alignment of large language models, and he has published more than 40 papers in top international conferences such as ACL, EMNLP, ICLR, etc. He has been cited more than 2,600 times in Google Scholar, and his H-index = 21. He has been awarded the Outstanding Graduation Thesis of Nanjing University, National Scholarship, Max Planck Society D. Scholarship, Outstanding Privately Financed International Student Scholarship, COLING2020 Best Demo Paper Award, ACL2023 Outstanding Paper Award and Best Topic Paper Award. He is a committee member of several top conferences and journals including ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, AAAI, TOIS, a domain chair of ACL Q&A Directions, and a theme editor of High-Performance Computing for AI in Big Model Era. The lab has deep communication with the corporate world, participating in several projects with WeChat AI and Alibaba, and co-publishing 7 top conference papers. It has collaborative projects with several top research organizations such as MILA, Google, Meta, Salesforce, etc., which provide abundant internship opportunities.
